### 🚀 ISRO * **DoS/ISRO Student Project & Internship**: Offers undergrad B.E/B.Tech internships for project work (60%+ aggregate) — duration varies (usually 1 to 4 months). You gotta apply via your college, not directly online ([isro.gov.in][1], [isro.gov.in][2]). * **Research Internship (RI)**: B.E/B.Tech students can do 4–6‑month internships (no stipend) — but only through academic institutions ([isro.gov.in][3]). **🔗How to apply**: Connect with your college research cell or department, they'll forward your application to ISRO via the official process (forms available on ISRO internship pages). --- ### DRDO * **DRDO Student Internship Scheme**: Offers 4 weeks to 6 months training across labs. They also run summer training programs in specific labs ([drdo.gov.in][4], [drdo.gov.in][5]). * Labs like GTRE in Bengaluru recently invited applications for graduate apprenticeship – typically 6–12 months, but some shorter gigs happen ([drdo.gov.in][6]). **🔗How to apply**: Check your preferred DRDO lab’s “Career” > “Student Training/Internship” section on drdo.gov.in and apply through their portal. --- ### NIC / Digital India * **NIC “INTERN” portal**: Ministry-run internship program (mainly for rural/non‑tier‑I women). Application form and eligibility details are on the site ([wcd.intern.nic.in][7], [wcd.intern.nic.in][8]). * **Digital India Internship** too exists with guidelines and registration ([dii.nic.in][9]). **🔗How to apply**: Visit “INTERN” site or digitalindia.gov.in, register, fill form. --- ### NIELIT * **Internship & training programs** in Embedded Systems, VLSI, IT, AI, Cybersecurity, Data Analytics etc. from 2 weeks up — some are hybrid and running now ([nielit.gov.in][10], [nielit.gov.in][11], [nielit.gov.in][12], [nielit.gov.in][13], [nielit.gov.in][14], [nielit.gov.in][15]). **🔗How to apply**: Head to nielit.gov.in → sections like “Haridwar” or “Calicut” → links to apply are active in those pages/PDFs. --- ### C‑DAC * Offers domain‑specific internships (e.g. SCADA, Ethical Hacking, AI) at various centres (Chennai, Pune, Kolkata). Internship durations are typically 2–3 months ([cdac.in][16], [cdac.in][17], [cdac.in][18], [cdac.in][19], [cdac.in][20]). **🔗How to apply**: Scroll to “Academic/Research” or “Student feedback” section on cdac.in. Most centres publish PDF adverts — download and follow the instructions or contact the centre directly. --- ### STPI * **STPI/STPINEXT internships** for undergrad/grad students with rolling opportunities. Chennai branch recently posted current openings ([chennai.stpi.in][21], [stpi.in][22], [stpi.in][23], [iotopenlab.stpi.in][24]). **🔗How to apply**: chennai.stpi.in → Career → STPI Internship. Fill the application form, check last date. --- ### CERT-In, BEL, ECIL, BHEL, AAI, CEERI, IIIT, TIFR * **CERT‑In**: No internships listed — only a permanent position for Private Secretary ([cert-in.org.in][25], [cert-in.org.in][26]). * **BEL**: Apprenticeships (1 yr) available; short internships unlisted currently ([bel-india.in][27], [bel-india.in][28], [bel-india.in][29]). * **ECIL**, **BHEL**, **AAI**, **CEERI**, **IIIT**, **TIFR**: I didn’t spot active internship pages (may be offline or not clearly indexed). You’ll likely have to check their “Career” or “Training” section manually. --- ### TL;DR **ISRO** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: 1–6 months 📌 Apply: Through college via ISRO website **DRDO** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: 1–6 months 📌 Apply: Via the specific lab’s student training portal **NIC** ✅ Available *(only for women from rural/non-tier I areas)* ⏳ Duration: Quarterly batches 📌 Apply: Register via IN‑INTERN portal **Digital India** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: Varies 📌 Apply: Register on digitalindia.gov.in **NIELIT** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: 2 weeks to 6 months 📌 Apply: Via official NIELIT site under respective programme pages **C-DAC** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: Around 2–3 months 📌 Apply: Check PDF notifications on centre-specific pages **STPI** ✅ Internship Available ⏳ Duration: Varies 📌 Apply: STPI career section **CERT-In** ❌ No internships listed currently ⏳ Duration: – 📌 Apply: Not available **BEL** ✅ Only for Apprenticeships (not short-term internships) ⏳ Duration: 1 year 📌 Apply: Through BEL career/apprenticeship portal **Others (ECIL, BHEL, AAI, CEERI, IIIT, TIFR)** 🤷‍♂️ Internship info not clearly listed ⏳ Duration: Unknown 📌 Apply: Manually check “Career” or “Training” sections on their websites --- [1]: https://www.isro.gov.in/InternshipAndProjects.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "DoS/ISRO INTERNSHIP & STUDENT PROJECT TRAINEE SCHEMEs" [2]: https://www.isro.gov.in/Internship.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Internship -
